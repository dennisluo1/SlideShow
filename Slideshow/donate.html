Make A Difference

Your donation to the Global Educational Exchange supports foreign volunteers who participate in teaching a foreign language in American classrooms. In the long run, students leave with a impactful foreign language class and volunteers leave with a time well spent learning about a culture other than their own. We see this as a mutual benefit that begins with your helping hand.

How Your Donation Helps:

    Allows students to interact with foreign language volunteers
    Build strong relationships amongst individuals from around the world and eventually create stronger ties amongst nations around the world
    Increase fluency amongst students taking foreign-language classes in inner-city schools
