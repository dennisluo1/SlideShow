The Need For Cultural & Linguistic Education

USA Statistics

The United States of America currently is the second largest Spanish-speaking country in the world after Mexico. This is taken into consideration individuals 5 years and older. It is estimated by the U.S Census Office that by 2050, there will be 132.8 million Spanish speakers in the country. However at the moment only 1% of American adults are proficient in a foreign language studied in school. We want to increase this low percentage of foreign language proficiency.


Benefits of Learning Another Language

The U.S Census Office estimates that by 2050, one in five jobs will be tied to international trade. This indicates that the need to speak another language other than English is increasingly significant for international business.

Physical and mental benefits
