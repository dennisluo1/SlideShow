How To Be Part Of It

At the Global Educational Exchange, we believe in the importance of cultural education. We believe that every individual can learn another language and at the heart of everything we do, we want to bring foreign volunteers to American under-privileged inner-city schools to increase cultural awareness and to teach students a secondary language.
